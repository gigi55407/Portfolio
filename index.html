About Me

Hi! My name is Jennifer Wandersee, and I recently completed a coding bootcamp at the University of Minnesota and look forward to using my skills in a web developer position. Through the bootcamp I have gained knowledge and experience in full stack web development, including html, cascading style sheets, javascript, jQuery, Node, GitHub and GitBash. I used these skills to complete individual projects and to collaborate with others in group projects. Please visit my portfolio page to see examples of my work.

In addition to web development skills, I have 15 years' experience as a technical writer, including creating manuals, stand-alone pieces, online job aids and eLearning modules. Although I am primarily focused on leveraging my coding skills, I would be glad to contribute my technical writing skills to projects if the need arises in my next position.

When I'm not working on coding or writing projects, I enjoy spending time in nature, taking part in musical pursuits, gardening, and visiting with friends at my neighborhood cafe.
